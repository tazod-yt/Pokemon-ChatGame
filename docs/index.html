Pokémon ChatGame

v1.0.5

Play Pokémon directly in the Stream Chat!

Catch wild spawns, level up, battle other viewers, and trade your creatures in real-time. Use the commands below to begin your journey!

Interactive Commands Reference

Click any category to filter, and click the copy icon on any command to copy it instantly!

Catch
!catch 📋

Catch the currently active wild Pokémon using an unlimited, standard Poke Ball.

Example: !catch
Great Ball
!catch great 📋

Catch the active spawn using a Great Ball (consumes 1 from bag, gives 1.5x catch success multiplier).

Example: !catch great
Ultra Ball
!catch ultra 📋

Catch the active spawn using an Ultra Ball (consumes 1 from bag, gives 2.0x catch success multiplier).

Example: !catch ultra
Challenge
!battle @user [pokemon/number] 📋

Challenge another viewer to a battle using a specific Pokémon from your inventory (can be name, species number, or PID).

Example: !battle @tazod Jolteon
Accept Battle
!accept @user [pokemon/number] 📋

Accept a pending battle challenge from a trainer using one of your Pokémon.

Example: !accept @ankit P3
Stats
!stats [pokemon/PID] 📋

Check ELO, levels, wins/losses, IV stats, and unique PIDs for a specific creature or your entire collection of that species.

Example: !stats Jolteon or !stats P149
Pokedex
!pokedex 📋

Get a summary of your Pokémon collection progress, total wins, losses, overall win rate, and total unique caught species.

Example: !pokedex
Bag
!bag 📋

Check all items currently held in your bag (evolution items, stones, great-balls, and ultra-balls).

Example: !bag
Use Item
!use [item_name] [PID] 📋

Use an evolution stone or item (like a Fire Stone or Metal Coat) from your bag on a Pokémon (must be at least Level 10) to evolve it.

Example: !use fire-stone P1
Trade
!trade @user [pokemon/PID] 📋

Send a trade proposal offering a Pokémon. You can specify by name/species number (if unique) or PID (if you own duplicates).

Example: !trade @tazod Jolteon
Accept Trade
!accepttrade @user [pokemon/PID] 📋

Accept a pending trade from another trainer and specify what Pokémon you are giving in return.

Example: !accepttrade @ankit Growlithe
Leaderboard
!leaderboard 📋

See the top trainers on the stream ranked by their ELO score.

Example: !leaderboard
